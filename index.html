I do Heena as per my client's requests and designs.

Growing up I always had a keen eye for art. I always made little sketches here and there, just for fun. With COVID and lock down, we were all forced to stay back at home. Just to keep myself busy, this time round, I ventured into Arabic calligraphy,at first, I had no plans to make this a business, but with my first art work being loved and family members offering to purchase, I thought why not try? That's when I started my Instagram page. I have received immense amount of love and support from family, friends and strangers. I ventured into Arabic calligraphy, as a Kenyan, I intend to increase awareness use Arabic calligraphy as a form of expression and widely used across the country.
